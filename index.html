About Kushi Cabs

Kushi Cabs is an innovative mobility platform that revolutionizes urban transportation through a comprehensive mobile application designed for drivers, vendors, and administrators. Our platform combines cutting-edge technology with user-friendly design to create a seamless experience for all stakeholders.

Real-Time Trip Management

Accept and track trips with live GPS navigation

Secure Wallet System

Manage earnings with transparent transactions

Performance Dashboard

Track statistics, ratings, and earnings in real-time

Self-Verification

Upload required documents (License, Vehicle, Aadhar) for quick approval

Emergency Support

Account Privacy

Multiple Payment Methods

Wallet Management
