Cancellation Policy

Trip Cancellation Guidelines

Effective Date: June 2026

1. Driver Cancellation

Policy:

  • Drivers can cancel trips up to 2 minutes after acceptance
  • After 2 minutes: 50% cancellation charge
  • After 10 minutes: Full fare charged as cancellation fee
  • Multiple cancellations may result in account suspension

Penalty System:

Cancellations Per Week Penalty
1-2 cancellations Warning
3-5 cancellations 10% commission deduction
6+ cancellations 20% commission deduction
Repeated violations Account suspension (3-7 days)

2. Vendor Cancellation

Policy:

  • Vendors can cancel trips up to 5 minutes after posting
  • Between 5-15 minutes: 25% trip value charged
  • After 15 minutes: 50% trip value charged
  • After 30 minutes (driver at pickup): 100% trip value charged

3. Cancellation Charges Breakdown

Who Cancels Timeline Charge
Driver First 2 minutes Free
2-10 minutes 50% of estimated fare
After 10 minutes 100% of estimated fare
Vendor First 5 minutes Free
5-15 minutes 25% of trip value
15-30 minutes 50% of trip value
After 30 minutes 100% of trip value

4. Dispute Resolution

If you dispute a cancellation charge:

  1. Contact support within 7 days
  2. Provide trip ID and screenshot
  3. Support team investigates
  4. Decision communicated within 48 hours
  5. If approved, refund issued within 5 days

5. Special Circumstances

  • Network Issues: System-determined penalties waived
  • Natural Disasters: All charges waived
  • Government Restrictions: Cancellations allowed without penalty
  • App Crashes: Support team reviews case-by-case
  • Safety Emergencies: All charges waived
