Refund Policy

Refund Process & Guidelines

Effective Date: June 2026

1. Refundable Scenarios

A. Automatic Refunds (Within 1 Hour)

  • Trip not completed (driver no-show)
  • Trip cancelled before pickup
  • Double charged for same trip
  • System error/technical glitch
  • App crash during transaction

B. Manual Refunds (Within 24-48 Hours)

  • Incorrect fare calculation
  • Disputed service quality
  • Payment processing errors
  • Duplicate payments
  • Unauthorized charges

C. No Refund Scenarios

  • Completed trips as requested
  • Voluntary trip cancellation after minimum time
  • Cancellation charges (non-refundable)
  • Promotion/discount codes misused
  • Wallet credit withdrawals

2. Refund Timeline

Scenario Processing Time
Double charge 1 hour (automatic)
No-show driver 2-4 hours
System error 24 hours
Disputed charge 48 hours
Appeal decision 72 hours

3. Refund Methods

1. Kushi Cabs Wallet (Instant)

  • Credited immediately after approval
  • Can be used for future trips
  • Can withdraw to bank anytime

2. Original Payment Method (24-48 hours)

  • For wallet withdrawals
  • Processed through Razorpay
  • May take additional 2-3 banking days

3. Bank Direct Transfer (3-5 business days)

  • For amounts over ₹5,000
  • Contact support for bank transfer option
  • Requires account verification

4. Partial Refunds

Partial refunds are issued for:

  • Partial trip completion: 50% refund
  • Excessive wait time: 25% refund
  • Late arrival at pickup: 10-30% refund
  • Service quality issues: 10-50% refund

5. Dispute Resolution Process

Level 1: In-App Support (24 hours)

  • Chat with support team
  • Provide evidence and explanation
  • Initial resolution offered

Level 2: Email Escalation (48 hours)

  • Escalate to support@kushicabs.com
  • Include trip ID, screenshots, details
  • Support manager reviews

Level 3: Super Admin Review (72 hours)

  • Final review by platform administrator
  • Decision is binding
  • Formal response issued
