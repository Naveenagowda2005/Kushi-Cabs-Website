Safety Guidelines

Your Safety is Our Priority

Last Updated: June 2026

Platform Safety Commitment

Kushi Cabs is committed to providing a safe, secure, and respectful environment for all users. These guidelines protect drivers, vendors, passengers, and the platform community.

1. Behavior Standards

Mandatory Requirements:

  • Treat all users with respect and dignity
  • Follow traffic laws and safety regulations
  • Maintain appropriate professional conduct
  • No discrimination or harassment
  • No intoxication while using platform
  • No weapons or dangerous items

Prohibited Behaviors:

  • Harassment, abuse, or threats
  • Sexual advances or inappropriate comments
  • Discrimination based on race, caste, religion, gender
  • Intimidation or coercion
  • Possession of illegal drugs or weapons
  • Reckless driving or traffic violations
  • Fraudulent activities or scams

2. Driver Safety Responsibilities

Vehicle Maintenance:

  • Regular vehicle inspection and maintenance
  • Valid insurance and registration
  • Brakes, tires, lights in working condition
  • Clean and hygienic interior
  • First aid kit and emergency tools
  • Emergency contact visible

Driving Standards:

  • Follow speed limits and traffic rules
  • Avoid reckless, dangerous driving
  • No mobile phone use while driving
  • Maintain full attention on road
  • Refuse to drive if fatigued
  • Refuse intoxicated or abusive passengers

Personal Safety:

  • Keep valuables secure
  • Verify passenger identity
  • Cancel if safety concerns
  • Report suspicious behavior
  • Always share location with emergency contacts
  • Maintain communication channel

3. Vendor Safety Responsibilities

Trip Information Accuracy:

  • Provide correct pickup/drop locations
  • Accurate trip duration estimate
  • Correct payment information
  • No false trips or test bookings
  • Cancel promptly if plans change

Behavior Expectations:

  • Respect driver's vehicle and property
  • Follow driver's reasonable instructions
  • No eating/smoking without permission
  • No loud music or disturbance
  • Respectful behavior toward driver
  • Payment without disputes

4. Reporting Unsafe Behavior

How to Report:

1. Immediate Safety Threat:

  • Call local police (100)
  • Alert platform (SOS button in app)
  • Ensure personal safety first

2. In-App Reporting:

  • Complete trip to access "Report" option
  • Select report category
  • Provide detailed description
  • Upload screenshots/evidence if available
  • Submit for investigation

3. Direct Support:

  • Email: safety@kushicabs.com
  • Phone: +91 7760322345 (24/7)
  • In-App Chat: Priority urgent support

5. Investigation Process

Timeline Action
0-2 hours Safety threat investigation
2-24 hours Serious allegations review
24-72 hours Regular complaint investigation
3-7 days Final decision and action

Possible Outcomes:

  • Warning issued to violating user
  • Account suspension (temporary)
  • Account termination (permanent)
  • Referral to law enforcement
  • Support services offered to victim

6. Account Security

Protect Your Account:

  • Never share OTP or passwords
  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication when available
  • Don't use public WiFi for sensitive actions
  • Log out after each session
  • Report suspicious login attempts
  • Update contact information regularly

7. Fraud Prevention

Red Flags - Never:

  • Share personal documents with unverified parties
  • Pay outside the app
  • Disclose banking information
  • Accept offers to bypass platform fees
  • Engage in rate manipulation
  • Create multiple accounts

If You Suspect Fraud:

  • Screenshot evidence
  • Don't respond to suspicious messages
  • Report to platform immediately
  • Contact local authorities if applicable
  • Monitor account activity
